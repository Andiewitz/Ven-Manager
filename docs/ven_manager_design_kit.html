Design Rationale

Dark ink sidebar + warm linen content = authority without coldness. Clients feel like they've entered a serious professional's workspace, not a startup dashboard.

Antique gold as accent signals premium without being showy. It's not the main event — it punctuates. The badge on the nav item, the CTA button, the invoice amount.

Serif display + mono metadata is intentional tension. Warm vs precise. Human voice + system detail. Exactly what a client-facing tool needs to feel like.
